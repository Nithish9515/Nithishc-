A diagnosis of typhoid fever can usually be confirmed by analysing samples of blood, poo, or pee. 
These will be examined under a microscope for the Salmonella typhi bacteria that cause the condition. 
A Widal test meaning is a sersology blood test that helps detect typhoid or enteric fever in the body. 
This test was first conducted in 1896 by Georges Ferdinand Widal and was named after himThe bacteria aren't always detected the first time, so you may need to have a series of tests.
